Adobo Recipe

Prep Time:

10 minutes

Cook Time:

45 minutes

Servings:

4 people

Ingredients:

  • 750g / 1.5 lb chicken thigh fillets (boneless and skinless)
  • 3 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1/3 cup (85ml) soy sauce (ordinary all-purpose or light)
  • 1/3 cup + 2 tbsp white vinegar
  • 4 bay leaves (fresh) or 3 dried
  • 2 tbsp oil (vegetable, canola, or peanut)
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp whole black pepper
  • 2 green onions/scallions (sliced for garnish)

Equipment:

  • Large skillet or Dutch oven
  • Mixing bowl
  • Measuring cups and spoons

Steps:

  1. Marinate the Chicken: Combine the chicken, soy sauce, and garlic in a bowl. Marinate for at least 20 minutes or up to overnight.
  2. Sear the Chicken: Heat 1 tbsp oil in a skillet over high heat. Remove chicken from marinade (reserve marinade) and place in the pan. Sear both sides until browned â€“ about 1 minute on each side. Do not cook the chicken all the way through.
  3. Cook Aromatics: Remove chicken from skillet and set aside. Heat the remaining oil in the skillet. Add garlic and onion, cook for 1 1/2 minutes.
  4. Simmer the Sauce: Add the reserved marinade, water, sugar, and black pepper. Bring it to a simmer then turn heat down to medium-high. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Cook Chicken in Sauce: Add chicken smooth side down. Simmer uncovered for 20 to 25 minutes (no need to stir), turning chicken at around 15 minutes, until the sauce reduces down to a thick jam-like syrup.
  6. Thicken Sauce: If the sauce isn't thick enough, remove chicken onto a plate and let the sauce simmer by itself - it will thicken much quicker - then return chicken to the skillet to coat in the glaze.
  7. Serve: Coat chicken in glaze then serve over rice.
