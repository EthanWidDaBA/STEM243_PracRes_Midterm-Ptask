Prep and Cook Time:

  • Prep: 5 minutes
  • Cook: 15 minutes
  • Total: 20 minutes
  • Servings: 10 people

Ingredients:

  • 10 pieces saba bananas
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 cups vegetable cooking oil

Equipment:

  • Large wok or deep pan
  • Fry skimmer strainer or colander
  • Paper towels
  • Bamboo skewers

Procedure:

  1. Heat a large wok or deep pan over high heat and pour in 2 cups of vegetable cooking oil.
  2. When the oil is hot, drop the bananas into the oil and fry for about 2 minutes or until they start to slightly brown.
  3. Sprinkle 1/4 cup of brown sugar into the wok and let it stand without stirring for a few moments to start caramelizing.
  4. Once the sugar starts to caramelize, gently stir the bananas to ensure they are coated with the caramelized sugar.
  5. Continue frying, stirring occasionally, until the bananas are fully coated and golden brown. Be careful not to overcook.
  6. Drain the bananas in a fry skimmer strainer or colander lined with paper towels to remove excess oil.
  7. Skewer two bananas per bamboo stick while they slightly cool down.
  8. Serve immediately.
