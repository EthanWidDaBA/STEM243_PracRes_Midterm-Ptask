Banana Cue Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 6 ripe saba bananas
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 cups cooking oil
  • Skewers

Instructions:

  1. Heat the cooking oil in a deep pan over medium heat.
  2. Roll the bananas in brown sugar until fully coated.
  3. Fry the bananas in the hot oil until the sugar caramelizes.
  4. Remove the bananas and let them cool slightly before skewering.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
