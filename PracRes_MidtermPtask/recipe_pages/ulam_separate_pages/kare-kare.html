Kare-Kare

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs oxtail or beef
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup ground rice
  • 1 eggplant, sliced
  • 1 banana heart, sliced
  • 1 cup string beans
  • 4 cups water
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup bagoong (fermented shrimp paste) for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, boil the oxtail or beef until tender. This may take about 1-2 hours.
  2. Once tender, remove the meat and set aside. Reserve the broth.
  3. In the same pot, add the peanut butter and ground rice to the reserved broth. Stir until well combined.
  4. Add the cooked meat back into the pot and simmer for about 10 minutes.
  5. Add the eggplant, banana heart, and string beans. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot with bagoong on the side.

Tips

For a richer flavor, you can add a bit of coconut milk. Serve with steamed rice for a complete meal.
