Sinigang

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pork (or shrimp/fish)
  • 6 cups water
  • 1 medium onion, quartered
  • 2 medium tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 radish (daikon), sliced
  • 1 cup green beans
  • 1 cup water spinach (kangkong)
  • 2-3 green chili peppers
  • 1/2 cup tamarind paste (or fresh tamarind)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation Steps

  1. In a pot, boil the water and add the pork (or shrimp/fish). Cook until tender.
  2. Add the onion and tomatoes, and simmer for about 10 minutes.
  3. Add the radish and green beans, and cook for another 5-7 minutes.
  4. Stir in the tamarind paste and green chili peppers. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper.
  5. Finally, add the water spinach and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with rice.

Serving Suggestions

Enjoy Sinigang with steamed rice and a side of fish sauce for dipping.
