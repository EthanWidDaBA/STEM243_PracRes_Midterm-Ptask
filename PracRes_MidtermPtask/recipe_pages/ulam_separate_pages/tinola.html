Tinola

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, sliced
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 green papayas, peeled and sliced
  • 2 cups spinach or chili leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a pot, heat oil over medium heat. Saut√© onion, garlic, and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add chicken pieces and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Pour in water and bring to a boil. Simmer for about 30 minutes or until chicken is cooked through.
  4. Add the green papaya and cook until tender.
  5. Stir in spinach or chili leaves and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve hot with rice.

Notes

Tinola is best served with steamed rice and a side of fish sauce for dipping.
